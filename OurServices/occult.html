Frequently asked question

Occult science is the study and application of esoteric knowledge and practices, including magic, astrology, alchemy, divination, and spiritualism, which are aimed at understanding and manipulating the hidden forces of the universe.

Magic is a part of occult science; it’s the practice within occultism that focuses on influencing the natural world through mystical, paranormal, or supernatural means. Occult science encompasses magic but also includes other practices and fields of study.

Occult science and religion can overlap but are not inherently the same. Some occult practices are integrated into religious rituals, while others are purely secular or belong to specific mystical traditions outside mainstream religions.

Mainstream science does not recognize occult science as a valid scientific discipline due to its reliance on supernatural explanations and lack of empirical evidence according to scientific standards. However, it’s studied as a cultural and historical phenomenon.
