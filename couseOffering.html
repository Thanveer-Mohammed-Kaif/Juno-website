Courses Offered

LEVEL 1 - Reiki First Degree Course

In this introductory Reiki course, students are initiated into Reiki energy healing. You’ll experience a deep chakra cleansing and learn how to channel healing energy to your own body. This foundational training sets the stage for your personal growth and a lifelong practice of self-healing.

Benefits of Reiki First Degree Course:

  • Balance Your Life: Reiki helps bring balance and alignment to your mind, body, and spirit, reducing the stress and distractions of modern life.
  • Physical Health: Reiki promotes natural healing, helping to boost your immune system and improve overall physical well-being.
  • Calm & Peace of Mind: Reiki assists in reducing anxiety, helping you maintain a calm and peaceful mindset amidst daily pressures.
  • Emotional Balance: With Reiki, you can more effectively manage emotions, creating a sense of stability and clarity.
  • Spiritual Clarity: Deepen your understanding of your life’s purpose and make meaningful progress in your spiritual journey.
  • Improved Relationships: Reiki supports emotional growth, leading to healthier relationships with others.

LEVEL 2 - Reiki Second Degree Course

Reiki Level 2 is perfect for those who have completed Level 1 and have practiced Reiki for at least 21 days. This advanced level introduces powerful tools like Distance Reiki and sacred Reiki symbols, enhancing your ability to heal both yourself and others more effectively.

Benefits of Reiki Second Degree:

  • Enhanced Healing Power: Your ability to channel Reiki energy increases significantly, allowing you to heal multiple people at once with more intensity.
  • Distance Healing: Learn to send Reiki energy to loved ones, clients, or even global communities, regardless of physical distance.
  • Healing the Past, Present, and Future: Reiki becomes a tool to heal trauma from the past, present challenges, and even prevent future blockages.
  • Inner Freedom: Reiki facilitates emotional and mental healing, helping you release past burdens and fostering a sense of emotional independence.
  • Improved Intuition: This level enhances your intuition, making your healing practice more effective and deeply connected.

LEVEL 3A - Reiki Master Healer

At the Master Healer level, students develop an advanced understanding of Reiki healing, equipping them to begin their professional healing career. This level introduces techniques that allow Reiki energy to flow effortlessly through your thoughts alone.

Eligibility: Completion of Level 2 with 21 days of consistent practice.

What You Will Learn:

  • Master-Level Energy Work: Reiki at this level is exponentially more powerful, enabling deep healing on physical, emotional, and spiritual levels.
  • Manifestation Power: Learn how to use Reiki to manifest positive outcomes by harnessing the full potential of your energy.
  • Spiritual Growth: This level also brings a deep spiritual transformation, increasing your capacity to heal others through thought alone
  • Professional Career: Begin your journey as a Reiki Master Healer, offering services to clients and starting your own holistic practice.

LEVEL 3B - Reiki Master Teacher

As a Reiki Master Teacher , you’ll be trained to pass on the Reiki healing art to others, empowering students to become Reiki healers themselves. This is a teacher’s level and involves a deep commitment to Reiki, healing, and mentorship.

Qualities of a Reiki Master Teacher:

  • Compassion & Empathy: A Reiki Master Teacher is committed to guiding students with compassion, love, and care.
  • Role Model: As a Master Teacher, you’ll serve as a beacon of Reiki practice and embody the principles of healing and wellness in your daily life.
  • Continued Mentorship: A key part of this role is providing ongoing support and guidance to students, even after their courses are completed.
  • Global Impact: Teach Reiki in your community or around the world, empowering others to start their own healing practices.

Diploma in Bach Flower Therapy

Bach Flower Remedies are a holistic approach to emotional healing, and in this diploma course, you will gain an in-depth understanding of the remedies, their applications, and how they can benefit yourself, others, and even animals.

Benefits of Bach Flower Therapy:

  • Natural Healing: This is a gentle, all-natural therapy that offers an alternative to conventional treatments, making it ideal for modern-day challenges.
  • Holistic Approach: Bach Flower Remedies address emotional and mental health, promoting overall well-being and peace of mind.
  • Versatility: Easily integrated into your existing wellness practices, Bach Flower Therapy can complement other therapies and offer effective results.
  • Safe for All: The remedies are safe for all ages, with no side effects, making them a trusted option for families and pets.
  • Emotional Empowerment: These remedies empower individuals to regain emotional balance, reduce stress, and face life’s challenges with strength.
